Tables of Time

Tim Berners-Lee WWW and HTML
Tim Berners-Lee was born in London, England, and studied physics at The Queen's College, Oxford, from 1973 to 1976. In 1989, he tested the first prototype of the World Wide Web, and by 1990, he had invented HTML, on which all of our websites run today.
Håkon Wium Lie CSS
Håkon Wium Lie was born in Norway in 1965, studying most notably at MIT. Håkon proposed the idea of CSS whilst working with Tim Berners-Lee, and later released CSS as a component of HTML in 1996.
Brenden Eich Javascript
Brenden Eich is from Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, and is known for creating Javascript, which he did in 1995. Eich is also well known for being the co-founder of the Mozilla Corporation. He studied at the Santa Clara University, in California, where he grew up.
